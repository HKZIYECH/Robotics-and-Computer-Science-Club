Our Courses

For the 2023/2024 academic year, we will be giving you guys a variety of courses to take, you can choose more than one course. As we said, coding cannot be mastered overnight but it can be achieved over time and dedicated practice.


We recommend that you enroll in less than three courses, at the end of the day it's your own personal preference but make sure you have enough time and dedication in order to get the best possible result.

1. Python

Python is a popular programming language known for its simplicity and readability. It's versatile, allowing developers to create anything from web applications to data analysis tools and artificial intelligence algorithms. Python emphasizes clean, concise code, making it accessible for beginners while offering powerful features for experienced programmers. Its extensive libraries and community support make it an excellent choice for both small projects and large-scale applications, enabling rapid development and easy collaboration.

2. Robotics

Robotics is the field of technology focused on designing, building, and programming robots to perform tasks autonomously or with human assistance. These robots can range from simple machines that perform repetitive actions in factories to complex systems capable of sensing and interacting with their environment. Robotics combines elements of mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, and computer science to create intelligent machines that can execute a wide variety of tasks, from assembly line work to exploring hazardous environments or even assisting humans in daily activities.

3. Website Development

Website development is the process of creating and building websites that are accessible on the internet. It involves designing the layout, structure, and appearance of the site, as well as coding the underlying functionality using programming languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Website developers work to ensure that the site is user-friendly, visually appealing, and functional across different devices and browsers. They may also integrate additional features such as forms, databases, or interactive elements to enhance the user experience. Overall, website development encompasses both the visual design and technical implementation required to bring a website to life on the web.

4. Game Development

Game development is the process of creating video games from concept to release. It involves designing the game's storyline, characters, and mechanics, as well as programming the code that brings these elements to life within a game engine. Game developers use various tools and technologies to build and test the game, incorporating graphics, sound, and other multimedia elements to enhance the player experience. Collaboration among designers, artists, programmers, and other team members is crucial throughout the development process to ensure the game meets its objectives and is engaging for players.

5. App Development

App development is the process of creating software applications that run on mobile devices like smartphones and tablets. It involves designing the user interface and experience, coding the functionality using programming languages like Java or Swift, and testing the app to ensure it works properly on different devices and operating systems. Developers may also integrate features such as GPS, camera access, or push notifications to enhance the app's capabilities. Throughout the development process, collaboration among designers, developers, and testers is essential to create a polished and user-friendly app that meets the needs of its target audience.

6. Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence (AI) is the development of computer systems that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. This includes tasks like recognizing patterns, understanding natural language, making decisions, and learning from experience. AI technologies use algorithms and data to mimic cognitive functions, enabling machines to process information, solve problems, and adapt to new situations. AI applications range from virtual assistants like Siri and Alexa to advanced systems in healthcare, finance, and autonomous vehicles, with the goal of improving efficiency, accuracy, and automation in various domains.

7. Graphic Design

Graphic design is the art of visually communicating ideas and messages using images, typography, and layout techniques. Graphic designers use their creativity and technical skills to create designs for various purposes, such as advertisements, logos, websites, and publications. They work with color, shape, and space to create visually appealing and effective designs that convey information and evoke emotions. Graphic design plays a crucial role in branding, marketing, and communication, helping businesses and organizations convey their identity and message to their audience in a visually compelling way.

8. Career Path

A career path in tech involves working in the field of technology, which includes various roles such as software development, cybersecurity, data analysis, IT support, and more. Individuals in tech careers use their skills in computer science, programming languages, problem-solving, and innovation to develop and maintain technological solutions that address specific needs or challenges. With rapid advancements in technology, a career in tech offers opportunities for growth, learning, and making a significant impact in various industries.
